How it works

Three simple steps to your next chat

  1. 1 Pick a mode from this page. Choose text, audio, video, or private rooms – each CTA sends you straight into the main app with the right option pre‑selected.
  2. 2 Fill in a quick, anonymous form. Choose a nickname, select how you identify, and share why you’re here – just enough context to get a better match.
  3. 3 Get connected and start talking. Our backend pairs you with someone compatible and opens the chat or call screen in just a second or two.

Designed for quick drop‑in chats

AnonKonnect works for quick check‑ins between tasks or longer conversations when you feel like staying. You’re always one click away from leaving or meeting someone new.

Trust & community

Anonymous doesn’t have to mean chaotic

Privacy‑first at every layer

The main app uses peer‑to‑peer WebRTC for media, which means your audio and video travel directly between participants whenever possible. We don’t run trackers or ad pixels on the chat surface.

  • ✓ No accounts or passwords: nothing to forget, nothing to recover.
  • ✓ Easy reporting tools: sessions that break the rules can be flagged from inside the call UI.
  • ✓ Short‑lived sessions: each connection is temporary by design.

Be kind, stay anonymous, move on

AnonKonnect works best when people show up curious and respectful. You always control your camera, mic, and whether you stay. When you’re done, just close the session and head back here for a new one.
